Welcome to all about lapland!

Every year, Santa spends a few weeks at his forest retreat to give his reindeer a good rest ahead of Christmas. The Santa Train takes children on a special journey all the way to the forest to meet Santa at his secret location. A magical experience they will always remember.

This is new and exclusive to the Santa Experience 2015. Each child can experience the true magic of Santa's factory, while creating their very own exclusive elf doll with the help of Santa's hardworking team of elves.

Snowy the Arctic owl shares his home with another 30 owls, hawks, and eagles at the Woodlands Falconry, the educational bird of prey centre here in Lapland. Come to visit Snowy and his friends this Christmas.
